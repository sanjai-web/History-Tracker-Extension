# Privacy Policy for Link Follow Extension

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-04

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
The **Link Follow Extension** is designed with privacy as a priority. 
- **No Personal Data Collection:** The extension does **not** collect, transmit, or share any of your browsing data, personal information, or usage statistics with the developer or any third parties.
- **Local Storage:** All recorded website links, titles, and timestamps are stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's local storage capabilities (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Data Access:** The extension accesses your tab information solely for the purpose of displaying your history within the extension popup and generating PDF exports at your request.

## 2. Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions to function:
- **`tabs` & `webNavigation`**: Used to detect when a new page is visited so it can be added to your local history log.
- **`storage`**: Used to save your history log on your device.

## 3. Data Retention
Because data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You can:
- Delete individual entries via the extension interface.
- Clear all data instantly using the "Clear All" button.
- Uninstalling the extension will permanently remove all associated data from your device.
